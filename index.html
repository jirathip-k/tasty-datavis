Introduction

Cooking is an essential part of life but it could be a hassle to organise grocery shopping lists and make a decision on recipes. This project could help making that decision easier.

Click on the widget to view the details of each widget or click on the question mark to view all the details.

Keep scrolling to view the narrative visualisation about ingredients paring.



Clustered of Ingredients

Each node allows you to hover to view the ingredient's name.

The ingredients are grouped by its community which can be thought of as a hidden grouping of similar ingredients. Therefore, these are unlabeled groupings of the ingredients.

Moreover, it can be seen that only the top twelve ingredients by its importance are highlighted by colour and icons. These ingredients will be explored in the next few visualisations.



Connected Ingredients

Ingredients are connected to the link that indicate pairs of ingredients that can be used to cook recipes. The more links connected to those ingredients the more important it is in contributing to high recipes' score which also means it has more centrality.

In this visualisation, centrality is normalised to maximum at one for most connected ingredients.

Moreover, each ingredient node can be hover to view its name and filter the links to only ones that are connected to that node.



Interesting facts for important ingredients

In case you forgot about what those ingredients are, the snippet of information from wikipedia for each important ingredients are provided.

These top important ingredients will be selected to explore their related recipes in the next two visualisations.



Recipes related to each ingredients

Faceted bar chart shows the number of positive ratings for top ten related recipes for each ingredient.

You can hover the charts to view recipes' information.



Which recipes are composed of more than one important ingredients?

It can be seen that most of the important ingredients are common in some of the recipes that are shown. These recommended recipes are generally have a good score and ratings has they composed of more than one important ingredients

The next visualisation will show another aspect by stacking the ingredients by recipes.



How many of the important ingredients in each recipe?

The visualisation provides information about common recipes for the important ingredients. The value of each ingredient in each recipe is simply one of it is in the recipe and zero if it is not in the recipe.

In this page, no user interaction is implemented.



What to cook?

Please hover at the ingredients' nodes to view recipe metrics that relate to the ingredient in the spider chart.

The metrics in the spider chart were scaled to minimum of zero and maximum of one hundred grouped by ingredients. For example, if the lowest recipe's score in that ingredient group is 90 then it will be shown as zero in the chart.

Moreover, you can hover at chart elements for each recipe to view recipe's name and unscaled metrics.

Note that some of the observations cook time is zero because it is missing.
